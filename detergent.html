Detergent



The main application of enzymes is in heavy-duty detergents for household laundry. The majority of enzymes used in laundry detergents are proteases for removing protein stains. They are often used in combination with amylases for removing starch. KLSs has successful launched protease and lipase for detergents.

The enzyme based detergents have better cleaning properties as compared to synthetic detergents. They are active at low washing temperatures and environment friendly also. The enzymes in the detergents do not lose their activity after removing stain. The enzyme containing detergents also improve the fabric quality and keeping color bright. The enzyme based detergents are used in small quantity as compared synthetic chemicals. They can work at very low temperature, environment friendly and completely biodegradable.
